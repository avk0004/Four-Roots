05 — FAQ

Common questions.

What is your typical project timeline?+

Standard custom builds typically take 3 Days — enough time for deep discovery, user-centric design, and pixel-perfect development without rushing quality.

How do you determine pricing?+

I provide value-based, fixed-price quotes. Every project is unique, so I price based on scope, complexity, and the business goals we aim to achieve together.

Will I own the final files?+

Yes, 100%. Upon completion you receive all source files, CMS access, and intellectual property rights. You own your digital assets with no hidden lock-ins.

Do you offer post-launch support?+

I stay close for 2 weeks post-launch for any adjustments. I also offer monthly maintenance retainers for ongoing updates, hosting, and security audits.

What technologies do you use?+

I focus on performance and maintainability — Next.js for speed, GSAP for premium motion, and Figma for design collaboration. Clean code is a standard, not an afterthought.

Can we work together remotely?+

Absolutely. Global collaboration is core to my work. I use Slack, Zoom, and Loom to ensure clear communication regardless of time zones.
